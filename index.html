The Other Side Of The World: Art and civilization of the global south

欢迎来到 Tribal Echo 社区，我们的网站源于对“全球南方” 视角的高度认同，这一概念直指那些历史上曾被边缘化，却沉淀着深厚文化能量的地区，而非洲面具作为南方国家中重要的代表，长久以来在主流叙事中始终处于被动被表述的地位。我们希望在此搭建一个观众与南方世界各文明之间的对话平台，借此消解文化隔阂最终推动一种基于平等与多 元的真正文化共生。

Tribes and masks

Baoule/巴乌勒族 Chokwe/乔科维族 Mende/门德族

The Goli dance of the Baoule tribe

Explore the rich cultural heritage and spiritual significance of the Goli dance, a traditional performance that embodies the Baoule people's connection to their ancestors, nature, and community. This ceremonial dance combines intricate movements, vibrant costumes, and rhythmic drumming to tell stories of harvest, celebration, and spiritual renewal.

The Mukanda ceremony of the Chokwe tribe

Explore the rich cultural heritage and spiritual significance of the Mukanda ceremony, a traditional initiation rite that embodies the Chokwe people's connection to their ancestors, nature, and community. This ceremonial practice combines intricate rituals, vibrant costumes, and rhythmic drumming to mark the transition from boyhood to manhood.

The Sowei mask and the Sande society

Discover the profound cultural significance of the Sowei mask within the Sande society, an exclusive women's society that embodies the Mende people's spiritual traditions, feminine power, and community values. This sacred mask represents ideals of beauty, morality, and social order in Mende culture.
